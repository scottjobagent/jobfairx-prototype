Troubleshooting Issues With JobFairX's Interview Platform

We're sorry you're having trouble connecting to JobFairX's interview platform. These problems are often caused by network settings, camera or microphone access, or browser compatibility. This article will show you how to fix each one.

Tip: Test your camera and microphone before the interview to make sure your permissions are set correctly.
Issue

Your camera and microphone are not working

Solution

Make sure your browser has permission to use your camera and microphone. Select Allow when prompted. If that didn't work, visit the Interview Test page and follow the instructions below:

Chrome
  1. Click or tap the camera icon in the address bar.
  2. Ensure camera and microphone permissions are set to Allow.
Safari
  1. Click the camera icon in the address bar.
  2. Set the camera and microphone to Always Allowed.
Edge
  1. Click the lock icon in the address bar.
  2. Set the camera and microphone to Allow.
Firefox
  1. On the test screen, click the lock or camera icon to ensure permissions are set to Allow.
Issue

Your browser isn't working properly

Solution

Use a supported browser. JobFairX's interview platform uses WebRTC technology which requires a modern browser.

Supported devices and browsers

  • Desktop or laptop: Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari
  • iPad or iPhone: Safari
  • Android: Chrome, Firefox

Incompatible devices and browsers

  • Internet Explorer
  • Chrome on iOS
Note: Using other apps during the interview may turn off the microphone if you're using a phone or tablet. If the interviewer can't hear you, restart your device and re-enter the interview.

For IT and network teams

Use this information to configure your network for JobFairX Interviews. JobFairX uses WebRTC for real-time video communication.

Step 1: Update your network's allowlist

Add these domains, protocols, and port ranges to your approved list (allowlist):

Domain Protocol Ports
*.jobfairx.com TCP 443
turn.jobfairx.com TCP, UDP 443, 3478
stun.jobfairx.com TCP, UDP 443, 3478
