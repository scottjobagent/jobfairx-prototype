3. Title

Format:

{City} Entry-Level Virtual Hiring Event - {EventDateShort}, {Year}

Example

Birmingham Entry-Level Virtual Hiring Event - June 25, 2026

Why this format

  • "Entry-Level Virtual Hiring Event" — owns a vertical Best Hire doesn't compete for. They're in-person + general.
  • City + Date in title — matches Best Hire's proven pattern. Captures "{city} job fair" search intent.
  • Hyphen separator + date — Eventbrite displays the date alongside the title anyway, but having it in the title boosts SEO match for "{city} job fair {month}" queries.

4. Summary

140 character max. Shown on event listing cards and category pages.

Get matched with employers hiring entry-level talent in {City}. Pre-scheduled video interviews. Free for job seekers.

Example (Birmingham, 132 chars)

Get matched with employers hiring entry-level talent in Birmingham. Pre-scheduled video interviews. Free for job seekers.

5. Cover image spec

One branded Entry-Level cover image, reused across every Entry-Level event regardless of city.

Specs

  • Dimensions: 2160 × 1080 px (Eventbrite recommended 2:1 ratio)
  • Format: JPG, < 10MB
  • Content: JobFairX brand blue (#0044B3) backdrop, photo of diverse early-career professionals at laptops/in interviews, "ENTRY-LEVEL VIRTUAL HIRING EVENT" wordmark, JobFairX logo
  • Text overlay rule: Keep text in the center 60% of the canvas — Eventbrite crops edges on different surfaces
  • City/date overlay: Not required. The Eventbrite title + date metadata handles this.
Asset to create: Design team needs to produce one master entry-level-cover.jpg at 2160×1080. Host at https://jobfairx.com/eventbrite-covers/entry-level.jpg (or wherever the CDN endpoint is). Reused for every Entry-Level event.

8. FAQ section

Eventbrite has a dedicated Frequently Asked Questions structured section, separate from the description body. The current Birmingham event puts all the FAQ content in the description — we move it here. Eventbrite's UI even pitches this with "Increase 8% organic traffic with FAQs."

Why this matters: FAQ items in the dedicated section get rendered with collapsible accordions, schema.org markup for search engines, and an SEO-friendly structure. Putting them in the description body gives up all of that.

FAQ items (moved from current description)

Each FAQ goes in as a separate Question / Answer pair via Eventbrite's structured component. Important: the existing FAQ block in the Birmingham event has 14 questions. We're keeping them as-is for content (battle-tested) but slimming the wording slightly.

  1. Q: How does JobFairX work for job seekers?
    A: JobFairX matches you with employers who have registered for the virtual hiring event. When you sign up and create a profile, we collect your qualifications, skills, and preferences. Our matching system identifies suitable job opportunities. If your qualifications align with a job, you receive an email invitation to schedule an interview with the employer during the event.
  2. Q: How do you match job seekers with employers?
    A: Our algorithm analyzes your skills, experience, location, and industry to identify suitable opportunities. If your qualifications align with a job, we send you an email invitation to schedule a video interview during the event.
  3. Q: How can I prepare for the virtual hiring event?
    A: (1) Update your profile with accurate skills, experience, and job preferences. (2) Make sure your resume is current. (3) Research the employer and the role. (4) Test your computer, internet, microphone, and camera ahead of time. (5) Dress as you would for an in-person interview.
  4. Q: Is there a fee to participate?
    A: No. Participating in JobFairX virtual hiring events and scheduling interviews with employers is 100% free for job seekers.
  5. Q: What should I do before the scheduled interview?
    A: Make sure you have a stable internet connection and a quiet, well-lit environment. Test your microphone and webcam. Review the employer and job requirements. Have your resume ready to share.
  6. Q: Can I conduct the interview using a mobile device?
    A: Yes, you can use a smartphone or tablet. A laptop or desktop is recommended for the best experience, but mobile works if your connection is stable.
  7. Q: What happens if I need to reschedule my interview?
    A: Log into your JobFairX dashboard and find your scheduled interview. There's an option to reschedule alongside the interview details. Let the employer know if possible.
  8. Q: When can I expect to hear back from the employer after the interview?
    A: Timing varies. Some employers give immediate feedback, others take days or weeks. If the employer gave a timeline during the interview, wait until that period passes before following up.
  9. Q: How do I participate in a JobFairX virtual hiring event?
    A: Register on Eventbrite, then complete your JobFairX profile. Our system matches your qualifications to employers, and if you match, you'll receive an email invitation with a scheduled interview time. You can only attend if you have a scheduled interview.
  10. Q: What are the timings for the event?
    A: {StartTime} to {EndTime} {Timezone}. Your specific interview time is assigned individually and communicated by email at least 24 hours before the event.
  11. Q: Can I attend from anywhere?
    A: Yes. JobFairX events are accessible from anywhere with an internet connection.
  12. Q: How do I access my scheduled interview?
    A: Log into your JobFairX account and go to your dashboard. Your scheduled interview details — including the date, time, and join link — will be there.
  13. Q: How does the interview process work?
    A: Interviews happen by video through the JobFairX platform. Log in about 5 minutes before your scheduled time. Most interviews last 15–20 minutes. Test your microphone and camera in advance. Dress professionally. Thank the interviewer at the end.
  14. Q: What if I face technical difficulties during the interview?
    A: Check your internet, refresh your browser, verify your microphone and camera are working. Try a different browser or device if needed. Contact JobFairX support if the issue persists.

11. Eventbrite API integration

Endpoints the developer will use

ActionEndpointMethod
Create event/events/POST
Update event/events/{event_id}/POST
Update event description (rich text body)/events/{event_id}/description/POST
Update event structured content (FAQ, Agenda)/events/{event_id}/structured_content/POST
Upload media (cover image)/media/upload/POST
Publish event/events/{event_id}/publish/POST

API docs reference

eventbrite.com/platform/docs — full API reference. The /events/{id}/structured_content/ endpoint is the one to study for the FAQ + Agenda sections — they use a Slate-like JSON format.

Suggested implementation pattern

// pseudocode
function createEntryLevelEvent({ city, state, eventDate, startTime, endTime, timezone, eventUrl }) {
  const vars = buildVariables({ city, state, eventDate, startTime, endTime, timezone, eventUrl });

  const event = await eb.post('/events/', {
    name: { html: render(TITLE_TEMPLATE, vars) },
    summary: render(SUMMARY_TEMPLATE, vars),
    start: { utc: toUTC(eventDate, startTime, timezone), timezone },
    end:   { utc: toUTC(eventDate, endTime,   timezone), timezone },
    online_event: true,
    listed: true,
  });

  await eb.post(`/events/${event.id}/description/`, {
    description: { html: render(BODY_TEMPLATE, vars) }
  });

  await eb.post(`/events/${event.id}/structured_content/`, {
    modules: [
      ...renderFAQModules(FAQ_ITEMS),
      renderAgendaModule(AGENDA_ITEMS, vars),
    ]
  });

  await eb.post(`/media/upload/`, { image_url: COVER_IMAGE_URL });
  await eb.post(`/events/${event.id}/publish/`, {});
  return event;
}
