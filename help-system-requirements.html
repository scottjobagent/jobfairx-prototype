System Requirements

Last updated: May 14, 2026

JobFairX is a web-based platform — there's nothing to install. To get the best experience managing your hiring events and interviewing candidates, make sure your setup meets the requirements below.

Device & Screen

Required device Laptop or desktop computer
Minimum screen width 1025 pixels or wider
Recommended 1280 × 800 or higher
Mobile & tablet Not supported The employer dashboard requires a desktop screen. If you access it on a phone or tablet, you'll see a message asking you to switch to a larger screen.
Why desktop only? Managing hiring events involves detailed tables, multi-step setup flows, and live video interviews with side panels for notes, chat, and resumes. These features need a full-sized screen to work properly.

Internet Connection

Dashboard & setup Any broadband connection (5 Mbps+)
Video interviews 10 Mbps or faster recommended for smooth video and screen sharing
Connection type Wired (Ethernet) or stable Wi-Fi. Avoid public or shared networks during live interviews.

Camera & Microphone

A camera and microphone are required for conducting live video interviews. You'll be able to test both on the pre-join screen before each interview starts.

Camera
Built-in webcam or external USB camera. Your browser will ask for permission the first time.
Required for: Live video interviews
Microphone
Built-in microphone, headset, or external USB mic. The pre-join screen includes a mic level test.
Required for: Live video interviews
Speakers or headphones
Any audio output device. Headphones are recommended to reduce echo during interviews.
Required for: Live video interviews
Browser permissions: When you start your first interview, your browser will ask to access your camera and microphone. Click "Allow" to proceed. If you accidentally block access, you can update your permissions in your browser's address bar (look for the camera or lock icon).

Pre-Interview Checklist

Before your first live interview, run through this quick checklist:

  • You're on a laptop or desktop computer with a screen wider than 1024 pixels
  • You're using a supported browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox)
  • Your internet connection is stable (10 Mbps+ recommended for video)
  • Your camera and microphone are connected and working
  • Browser permissions for camera and microphone are set to "Allow"
  • You've tested your setup on the pre-join screen before the interview starts

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use JobFairX on my phone or tablet?
The employer dashboard requires a desktop or laptop computer. Features like managing events, conducting interviews, and reviewing candidates need a full-sized screen to work properly. If you access the dashboard on a mobile device, you'll be directed to switch to a larger screen.
What if my camera or microphone isn't working?
First, check that your browser has permission to access your camera and microphone. Click the lock or camera icon in your browser's address bar and make sure both are set to "Allow." If they're allowed but still not working, try closing other apps that might be using your camera (like Zoom or FaceTime), then refresh the page.
Do I need to install any software?
No. JobFairX runs entirely in your web browser. There's nothing to download or install. Just sign in at jobfairx.com and you're ready to go.
Can I use a Chromebook?
Yes. Chromebooks run Google Chrome, which is our recommended browser. As long as your screen is wider than 1024 pixels and you have a working camera and microphone, you're all set.
What internet speed do I need?
For browsing the dashboard, setting up events, and managing applicants, any standard broadband connection (5 Mbps+) works fine. For live video interviews, we recommend 10 Mbps or faster for smooth, uninterrupted video. A wired Ethernet connection is ideal, but stable Wi-Fi works too.
