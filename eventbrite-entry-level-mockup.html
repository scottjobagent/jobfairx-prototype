Frequently asked questions

How does JobFairX work for job seekers?
JobFairX matches you with employers who have registered for the virtual job fair. When you sign up and create a profile, we collect your qualifications, skills, and preferences. Our matching system identifies suitable job opportunities. If your qualifications align with a job, you receive an email invitation to schedule an interview with the employer during the event.
How do you match job seekers with employers?
Our algorithm analyzes your skills, experience, location, and industry to identify suitable opportunities. If your qualifications align with a job, we send you an email invitation to schedule a video interview during the event.
How can I prepare for the virtual job fair?
Update your profile with accurate skills, experience, and job preferences. Make sure your resume is current. Research the employer and the role. Test your computer, internet, microphone, and camera ahead of time. Dress as you would for an in-person interview.
