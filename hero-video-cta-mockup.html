Spec

Hero CTA

  • Primary (unchanged): "Reserve My Spot" — solid blue pill (#2563eb), white text, soft shadow, arrow icon.
  • Secondary (new): "See how it works" — white pill, 1.5px gray border (#d1d5db), dark text. Small blue circle with play glyph on the left. Hover: border → #2563eb, slight lift.
  • Action: opens the video modal. No navigation.
  • Pricing access: top nav still has "Pricing" — no functional loss from dropping "View Packages".

Modal

  • Backdrop: rgba(10, 14, 26, 0.84), full screen.
  • Card: 16:9, max width 1100px, border radius 18px, large drop shadow.
  • Close: X button above-right of the card. Backdrop click also closes.
  • Player: same poster + center play button pattern as the in-page video would use.
  • Keyboard: ESC closes the modal. Focus traps within the modal when open.
  • Embed: swap the faux poster for an iframe (YouTube / Vimeo / Mux) when wiring up. Click on the play button starts playback; ideally autoplay on modal open so the user doesn't have to click twice.
