Notes

Design and font specification for the interview join flow, mobile and desktop. All sizes are in CSS pixels. Mobile type is fixed and holds from 360 px to 430 px wide. Desktop type scales per breakpoint. For a pixel-accurate reference, open the full-window preview pages (the Small / Medium / Large links next to each screen in the Desktop view); the board thumbnails are a scaled-down map (about 60%), not a measurement source.

Per-screen behavior

ScreenBehavior and content
LoaderAuto-advances (about 1.3s) to Get ready
Get readyGreen-room layout: hero camera preview with on-screen mic and camera toggles, device pills, then Continue
Interview statusOne screen, four states: Interviewer available, In line (queue position and estimated wait), Interviewer now available, No interviewer available. Content is centered
In interviewRemote video fills the frame, self-view in a corner tile, floating control bar. Mute, Video, More (Chat and Notes), and Leave all function
End confirmModal over the call: cannot rejoin once ended
RatingFour thumbs-up/down questions plus an optional note. Submit shows “Thanks for your feedback”; Skip shows “Your interview is complete”
Done“Taking you to your dashboard” then redirect
RejoinRe-entry screen shown only if the candidate disconnects during their interview slot (for example 2:00 to 2:30 PM)

Interviews run inside JobFairX. There are no external apps or links; the candidate joins the interview room in the browser.
