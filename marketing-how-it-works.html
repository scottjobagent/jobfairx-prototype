How Hiring Events Work

From candidate matching to hiring decisions — everything happens in one place.

1

Candidates matched to your open roles

Candidates register and request interviews based on your posted jobs. You'll see who's interested, review resumes, and accept or decline with one click. Message candidates directly if you have questions before the event.

Candidate matching Built-in messaging Resume preview
2

Interview-ready candidates

On event day, your lobby fills with confirmed candidates. See who's ready in real time, check their status, and start interviews with one click. Candidates who arrive early wait in a virtual waiting room until you're available.

Auto-scheduled interviews Live lobby management Real-time status tracking
3

Interview with everything you need

Join a video interview with the candidate's resume, your notes, and a chat window right alongside the call. No switching tabs or shuffling papers — everything is in one view so you can focus on the conversation.

Video interviews Side-panel resume Interview notes
4

Hire with confidence

After each interview, rate candidates as Yes, Maybe, or No and add notes while the conversation is fresh. When the event ends, you'll have a clear summary of every candidate to share with your team and make faster hiring decisions.

Candidate evaluation Event reporting Team collaboration
