Proposed: How it works
How JobFairX Works
Invite-only interviews, three steps
1

Register and build your resume

Create a free account and build your resume in minutes. That is all it takes to be seen.

2

Get matched and invited

Employers post roles and our system matches your resume, then emails you an invite to request an interview. No applications, no black hole.

3

Employer accepts, you interview

When the employer accepts, you get a confirmed time and interview by video right on JobFairX. No Zoom, no downloads.
