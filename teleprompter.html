JobFairX Demo Script v6
~2:40
1.0×
SECTION 1 Cold Open ~12s
"Hi, my name is Scott, I'm with JobFairX. I'm going to walk you through how a virtual hiring event works. Candidates are matched to your jobs and schedule interviews with you on JobFairX."
Stage direction Start on dashboard. Smile, casual energy. No screen action yet — just intro.
SECTION 2 Dashboard ~18s
Point at The Candidates column on the dashboard
"From your dashboard you have a global view — the event you're registered for, the jobs you're hiring for, and your candidate pipeline under Candidates, where you'll see how many are pending and how many are upcoming."
SECTION 3 Pre-Event Matching ~22s
"Once you post your jobs and set your interview settings, candidate matching activates automatically."
"Click Go to lobby to manage your candidates."
Switch to Lobby — Active view, Not yet interviewed tab
"From here, you can accept or decline each request, or turn on auto-accept so matched candidates are automatically scheduled."
SECTION 4 Lobby — Day of Event ~55s
"The day of your event, from your dashboard, click Go to lobby."
Point at The "Your status: Available / Away" toggle at the top
"First, you set your status to Available. That tells the system you're ready to start interviewing."
Point at Waiting rooms section
"When a candidate joins for their interview, they appear in the waiting rooms. You'll see who's waiting and the job they're interviewing for."
Point at Interview rooms section
"Once an interviewer is available, the candidate moves from the waiting room into the interview room. Click Start interview, and you're brought into the interview room where you interview the candidate."
After interview action Demonstrate the end-of-interview flow
"After each interview, you'll mark the candidate yes, no, or maybe, take your notes, and click End interview. That candidate moves to your Interviewed tab and you go back to Waiting to interview for the next one."
SECTION 5 Event Report ~22s
Navigate to Dashboard → Past events → click "View Event Report"
"After your event, you get a full report of every interview you conducted — how many you marked yes, maybe, and no. From here you can schedule follow-up interviews, message candidates, and download their resumes."
SECTION 6 Close ~18s
"That's how JobFairX helps you hire faster, lower your cost per hire, and connect with matched candidates ready to interview. Check out our 2026 hiring event calendar to see what's coming up across healthcare, technology, veterans, diversity, entry-level, and more."
