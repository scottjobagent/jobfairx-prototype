Healthcare hiring event demo

A 5–7 minute walkthrough of what a JobFairX healthcare hiring event looks like, from registration to event report. Built for cold outreach and post-call follow-ups with healthcare employers.

Employer in demo: Northstar HealthFeatured jobs: RN, Medical Assistant, Physical TherapistTotal runtime: ~6 min

1Pick your event

~0:45

What to show
  • Dashboard for Northstar Health with 3 upcoming events
  • Click "Register for an event" → show event picker with healthcare options across cities
  • Pick Dallas Healthcare Hiring Event, Apr 22
What to say
"You pick a hiring event in your city — healthcare events run roughly twice a month in major US and Canadian markets. You're not posting a job and waiting. You're picking a date when candidates show up to interview."

"You tell us what you're hiring for and what qualifies a candidate. Auto-accept means anyone who passes screening and picks an open slot is automatically confirmed — no manual scheduling, no email back-and-forth."

Recording tips

  • Open every URL in a separate tab before hitting record — you click between tabs, you don't navigate live.
  • On the dashboard, use the "Dashboard view" toggle in the header to flip between Active / Prepare / Live / Ended states without reloading.
  • On the lobby, use the "Mode" toggle to demo Active → Live → Event ended in one cut.
  • If the resume on the interview screen looks short, scroll the side panel to fill the moment with voiceover instead of clicking around.
  • Aim for under 7 minutes. Anything longer and prospects bail before the close.
